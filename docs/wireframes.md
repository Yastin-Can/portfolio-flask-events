# Wireframes Documentation for Portfolio Flask Events

## Overview
This document outlines the wireframes and design concepts for the Portfolio Flask Events project, aimed at attracting clients from the events sector and restaurants with outdated websites.

## Home Page
- **Hero Section**: A visually appealing hero image with a tagline that captures the essence of the services offered.
- **Call to Action**: A prominent button leading to the contact form.
- **Services Overview**: Brief descriptions of key services provided.

## Services Page
- **Service Cards**: Each service is represented by a card that includes an image, title, and short description.
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Clicking on a service card leads to a detailed description of the service.

## Portfolio Page
- **Project Thumbnails**: A grid layout showcasing thumbnails of completed projects.
- **Hover Effects**: Thumbnails change on hover to reveal project titles.
- **Case Study Links**: Each thumbnail links to a detailed case study page.

## Case Study Page
- **Project Details**: A detailed view of the project, including challenges faced, solutions provided, and client testimonials.
- **Image Gallery**: A gallery of images related to the project.

## Contact Page
- **Contact Form**: A user-friendly form for inquiries, including fields for name, email, message, and a submit button.
- **Contact Information**: Display of phone number, email, and social media links.

## 404 Page
- **Error Message**: A clear message indicating that the page was not found.
- **Navigation Links**: Links to redirect users back to the home page or other key sections of the site.

## Design Concepts
- **Color Palette**: A modern and inviting color scheme that reflects the brand identity.
- **Typography**: Use of clean and readable fonts for better user experience.
- **Responsive Design**: Ensuring the website is mobile-friendly and adapts to various screen sizes.

## Conclusion
These wireframes serve as a blueprint for the development of the Portfolio Flask Events website, ensuring a cohesive and user-friendly experience for potential clients in the events and restaurant sectors.
